# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I’m Chastity! I’m a beginner software engineer and the proud owner of Chasslayy Graphics, <br>a Black-owned graphic design business dedicated to empowering <br>small businesses in the fashion and beauty industry. At Chasslayy Graphics, I specialize<br> in crafting logos, branding, websites, and promotional materials that bring bold visions to life.<br>With a background in graphic design and a growing passion for software development, <br>I’m blending creativity and technology to build impactful projects. My GitHub features <br>work that reflects my interests and values, including a blog on natural hair care and <br>tools for the curly hair community. As a designer and developer, I’m committed to <br>creating innovative solutions that inspire and uplift others. Please look at my work<br> and join me on this exciting journey of creativity and growth!
